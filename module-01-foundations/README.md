# Module 01: Foundations & History
 
## What Stronghold Is
 
Stronghold is a financial infrastructure (fintech) company, not a blockchain. It positions itself as a platform connecting traditional payment rails (banks, ACH) with distributed ledger technology.

## The SHx Launch (2018)
 
Confirmed on the official source (`stronghold.co/shx` FAQ):
 
- SHx was issued in 2018.
- Approximately 5% of total supply was distributed as an airdrop to Stronghold's early user base at the end of 2018.
- No ICO, TGE, or IEO was conducted, a distinction Stronghold makes explicitly, likely to differentiate SHx from speculative crypto projects of that era.
- Fixed supply from origin: 100,000,000,000 SHx, with no additional issuance mechanism.

## From Rewards Token to Payments Infrastructure: StrongholdNET (2023)
 
Per an official BusinessWire press release (verified): Stronghold launched **StrongholdNET** as the umbrella brand for its full ecosystem (ACH, checkout, card processing, merchant financing, and SHx). The release quotes CEO Tammy Camp on the mission of making financial services accessible to all.
 
This marks a positioning shift: SHx moved from being primarily a loyalty rewards token to being the settlement layer of a broader payments infrastructure.
 
## 2025: The Multi-Chain Expansion Year (recent context, verified)
 
Confirmed through official 2025 press releases and blog posts:
 
- **June 2025**: launch of the 60B SHx escrow contract on Soroban (independently inspected on-chain for this knowledge base; see Module 04).
- **October 2025**: Kraken listing, SHx's first U.S. exchange listing.
- **November 2025**: launch of the SHx Bridge (Stellar to Ethereum via Axelar) and Uphold listing.
- Recognition: Forbes Fintech 50, 2025 Inc. 5000 list.
- Reported community size: over 215,000 global members (company-reported figure, not independently verified).
## The EDP Mandate (Ecosystem Development Program)
 
This is the direct link to our grant application, fully confirmed in official documentation (`docs.shx.stronghold.co/ecosystem/edp`):
 
> The EDP exists to prioritize and support initiatives that accelerate the development of the wider SHx ecosystem, including SHx-powered StrongholdNET integrations.
 
It offers two tracks: **Ecosystem Grants** (an ongoing open call for proposals) and **Bounties** (project-specific, currently none active).
